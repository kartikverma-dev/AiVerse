# Introduction

## Context and Background
The velocity of machine learning research since the transformer architecture\'s introduction in 2017 has led to an unprecedented rate of terminology creation. Concepts like Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Model Context Protocol (MCP), and Vibe Coding emerge and evolve within weeks. 

## Problem Description
Developers, academics, and industry strategists face a deluge of new terms, with no reliable mechanism to distinguish transient buzzwords from foundational shifts. Static glossaries fail to update at this pace, and raw search engines do not preserve lineage or logical dependencies.

## Scope of Work
CredgeAiVerse establishes a systematic, automated platform to record, analyze, and map the semantic relationships and lifecycles of machine learning paradigms.
