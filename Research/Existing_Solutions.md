# Existing Solutions

## Wikipedia
- **Strengths:** Highly trusted, community-curated, broad scope.
- **Weaknesses:** Extremely slow to adopt cutting-edge developer terms (e.g., "Vibe Coding" lacks a Wikipedia entry due to strict notable-source criteria).

## Hugging Face & Papers with Code
- **Strengths:** Excellent indexing of models, codebases, and leaderboards.
- **Weaknesses:** Focused on model benchmarks rather than the conceptual evolution and lineage of software paradigms.

## AI Newsletters and Aggregators
- **Strengths:** Fast reporting.
- **Weaknesses:** High noise-to-signal ratio, lack of structured relationships, and lack of historical continuity.
