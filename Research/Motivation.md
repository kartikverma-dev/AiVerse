# Motivation

The primary motivation of this research is to construct an infrastructure that democratizes AI understanding while tracking tech progression. By formalizing concept lineage (e.g., showing how *Prompt Engineering* laid the groundwork for *Agentic Coding* and *Loop Engineering*), we enable:
1. **Developers:** To focus learning on stable, high-value concepts.
2. **Researchers:** To map their work to existing conceptual taxonomies.
3. **Organizations:** To make technology adoption decisions based on empirical lifecycle metrics rather than social media hype.
