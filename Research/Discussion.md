# Discussion

## Findings Analysis
The results demonstrate that combining real-world metrics (GitHub stars, arXiv citations) with age heuristics is highly effective in separating hype from reality. Mismatches occurred in transition phases (e.g., RAG transitioning from growing to stable), which can be resolved by fine-tuning thresholds.

## Implications for Developers
Developers can trust the "stable" and "growing" badges to guide their study decisions, avoiding wasting hours on declining framework-specific terms.

## Data Quality Concerns
Relying on GitHub stars makes the system vulnerable to star-manipulation and artificial hype, which requires citation weighting factors.
