# Rejected Concepts & Ideas

- **Reject 1: User-created concepts without review.**
  - *Why:* Leads to duplicate, low-quality, and marketing-spam entries.
  - *Decision:* Replaced with a moderation flow where concepts must be approved by curators.
- **Reject 2: Auto-generating whole concepts on every search.**
  - *Why:* Extremely expensive API calls, slow response times, and potential AI hallucinations.
