# Current Status

## Why it exists
To outline what works, what is verified, and what is ready for production deployment.

## When it was introduced
Updated July 2026.

## What problem it solved
It provides clear visibility into current deployment readiness for product managers.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Stating status via Slack. Dismissed as disorganized.

## Why the final decision was chosen
A clear, living document updated after compiler runs is the single source of truth.

## Future possibilities
- Hooking status badges into deployment pipelines.
