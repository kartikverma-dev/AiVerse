# Development Timeline

## Why it exists
To record the phases of design, database seeding, feature implementation, and compilation checks.

## When it was introduced
Kept since inception.

## What problem it solved
It keeps developers and managers aligned on milestone achievements and launch dates.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Using Jira exclusively. Dismissed because markdown timelines in the repo are closer to the code.

## Why the final decision was chosen
A chronological developer log in the codebase ensures the history remains with the code.

## Future possibilities
- Integration of automated release notes generation.
