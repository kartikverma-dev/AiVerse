# Resume Bullet Points

## Why it exists
To translate engineering achievements on this project into high-impact professional metrics.

## When it was introduced
Created in 2026.

## What problem it solved
It helps contributing developers describe their work impact clearly on resumes.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Basic task descriptions. Dismissed because impact-driven metrics (compilation success, render speed) are more effective.

## Why the final decision was chosen
Well-formatted bullet points ensure engineers get credit for the complex architecture they built.

## Future possibilities
- Automated career portfolio generation templates.
