# Key Design Decisions

## Why it exists
To record why we chose specific colors, fonts, and animation properties.

## When it was introduced
Compiled in 2026.

## What problem it solved
It prevents designers and developers from making arbitrary styling changes that dilute the brand.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Using Tailwind default colors. Dismissed because default shades (like gray-900) look too generic.

## Why the final decision was chosen
A curated palette (Ink Navy #0E1018, Signal Gold #D9A85C) yields a unique, high-end editorial feel.

## Future possibilities
- Design system token compilation to JSON for multi-platform support.
