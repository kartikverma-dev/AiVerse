# Challenges Faced

## Why it exists
To list technical obstacles, such as hydration mismatches, D3 graph canvas sizes, and Tailwind v4 upgrades.

## When it was introduced
Documented throughout development.

## What problem it solved
It provides solutions for recurring developer pain points.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Ignoring minor console warnings. Dismissed because strict cleanliness is required for production stability.

## Why the final decision was chosen
Documenting issues and solutions builds team resilience and speeds up onboarding.

## Future possibilities
- Automated lint checking for common hydration issues.
