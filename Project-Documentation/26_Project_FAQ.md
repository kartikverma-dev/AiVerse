# Project FAQ

## Why it exists
To answer common user questions about data sources, accuracy, and status calculations.

## When it was introduced
Created in 2025.

## What problem it solved
It reduced support tickets and email queries from confused users.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Direct email support only. Dismissed as unscalable.

## Why the final decision was chosen
An on-site FAQ provides immediate answers and improves SEO.

## Future possibilities
- AI-powered chat assistant built on our own concept vector database.
