# Competitor Analysis

## Why it exists
To compare CredgeAiVerse with other resources (Wikipedia, Hugging Face Papers, arXiv, newsletters).

## When it was introduced
Compiled in 2025.

## What problem it solved
It identified that competitors are either too static (Wikipedia) or too raw (arXiv), leaving a gap for a curated, interactive graph.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Building on top of Wikipedia APIs. Dismissed due to licensing issues and slow adoption of cutting-edge tech terms on Wikipedia.

## Why the final decision was chosen
An independent, custom-built Next.js client allows rapid database seeding and specialized Framer Motion/D3.js visualizations.

## Future possibilities
- Web browser extensions to explain concepts on arbitrary webpages by querying our API.
