# Glossary of Project Terms

## Why it exists
To define internal project terms (e.g., Battle Mode, Curator, Sparkline, Trust Index).

## When it was introduced
Added in 2025.

## What problem it solved
It aligned design, business, and engineering teams on terminology.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Relying on external definitions. Dismissed because internal features have specific contextual definitions.

## Why the final decision was chosen
An internal glossary ensures absolute clarity within the development team.

## Future possibilities
- Embedding hover definitions directly in the admin panel.
