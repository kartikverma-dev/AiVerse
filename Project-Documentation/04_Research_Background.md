# Research Background

## Why it exists
To ground the platform in scientific literature regarding semantic networks, ontology engineering, and citation graphs.

## When it was introduced
Authored in late 2025.

## What problem it solved
It validated that mapping concept lineages matches established cognitive theories of learning and knowledge representation.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Relying entirely on casual tags. Dismissed as academically weak.

## Why the final decision was chosen
A parent-child graph schema (`concept_evolutions` table) replicates taxonomic hierarchies and citation flows.

## Future possibilities
- Generating visual path trees matching citation maps automatically.
