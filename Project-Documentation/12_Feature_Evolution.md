# Feature Evolution

## Why it exists
To document how features like Battle Mode and Term Origin Stories evolved over time.

## When it was introduced
Added in 2026.

## What problem it solved
It enhanced user engagement by moving beyond simple cards to comparative and etymological exploration.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Creating a separate video explanation portal. Dismissed because text with interactive toggles is faster to consume.

## Why the final decision was chosen
Providing structured comparisons and origin contexts matches the historical academic theme.

## Future possibilities
- Dynamic visual timelines for every single concept.
