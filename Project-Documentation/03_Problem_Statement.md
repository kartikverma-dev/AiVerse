# Problem Statement

## Why it exists
To articulate the core challenges in the AI learning ecosystem: hype, fragmentation, lack of historical lineage, and difficult technical barriers.

## When it was introduced
Drafted at inception in 2025.

## What problem it solved
It serves as the north star for product decisions, ensuring every feature directly addresses a defined user pain point.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Focusing solely on academic researchers. Dismissed because developers need this knowledge just as urgently.

## Why the final decision was chosen
A dual-depth approach (Beginner vs Technical) ensures the product serves both novices feeling overwhelmed and experts needing precision.

## Future possibilities
- Personalized learning paths matching the user's current baseline.
