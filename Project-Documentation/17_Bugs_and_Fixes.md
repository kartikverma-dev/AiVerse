# Bugs and Fixes

## Why it exists
To log significant bugs, such as click propagation in card toggles, and their resolutions.

## When it was introduced
Added in 2026.

## What problem it solved
It prevents regression bugs by documenting clear resolution pathways.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Relying on GitHub Issues only. Dismissed because issues are frequently closed and forgotten.

## Why the final decision was chosen
Keeping a dedicated log in the docs makes the solutions easily searchable.

## Future possibilities
- Automated integration testing for interactive elements.
