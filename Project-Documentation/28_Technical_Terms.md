# Technical Glossary

## Why it exists
To catalog key technological acronyms (MCP, RAG, MoE, KD, RLHF) used in our architecture.

## When it was introduced
Created in 2025.

## What problem it solved
It helps non-expert engineers and stakeholders understand technical discussions.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Expecting readers to look up terms on Google. Dismissed because standard definitions lack platform-specific context.

## Why the final decision was chosen
A centralized technical glossary ensures immediate comprehension.

## Future possibilities
- Linking terms automatically to definitions across all documentation files.
