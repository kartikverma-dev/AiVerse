# Market Research

## Why it exists
To analyze the target audience: AI developers, computer science students, and technical product managers who struggle with technical velocity.

## When it was introduced
Conducted in 2025.

## What problem it solved
It proved there is a massive market segment of developers who spend hours decoding new terms (like MCP or Vibe Coding).

## Alternative ideas discussed
- Targeting general business executives. Dismissed because their needs are high-level and better served by general newsletters.

## Why the final decision was chosen
Focusing on technical professionals and learners maximized product-market fit for a schema-rich, interactive app.

## Future possibilities
- Enterprise team spaces for internal concept mapping and tooling libraries.
