# Idea Generation

## Why it exists
To document the brainstorming sessions and initial sparks that led to the creation of the platform.

## When it was introduced
Documented during initial planning in 2025.

## What problem it solved
It prevented the team from forgetting the core mission: tracking the *evolution* of ideas rather than just cataloging them.

## Alternative ideas discussed
- A directory of AI companies. Dismissed because company names change, but underlying concepts (RAG, MoE, MCP) remain foundational.
- An AI code snippet repository. Dismissed because snippet sites are abundant; conceptual tracking is rare.

## Why the final decision was chosen
Focusing on the conceptual pedigree of ideas provided the highest long-term academic and practical value.

## Future possibilities
- Crowdsourced concept suggestions with curator validation queues.
